Welcome to Al Sahel Al Akhdar Sweets,
Established in Jordan since 1990, our family owned business has been serving delectable sweets with a legacy dating back to 1936. Originating in Palestine, Jerusalem, our journey began with our first shop and has since expanded to 17 branches.
Renowned for our mastery in both Eastern and Western sweets, we specialize in the art of Kunafa. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our unwavering dedication to offering superior quality at affordable prices.
Al Sahel Al Akhdar Sweets was founded by Ismael Al Ayyan, and our tradition of crafting indulgent delights continues to be cherished by generations of middle eastern sweets lovers.
